Dr. Sourav Kanti Addya

Assistant Professor

National Institute of Technology Karnataka, Surathkal

Dr. Addya is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the National Institute of Technology Karnataka Surathkal, India, where he manages the CS^3 Lab since 2019. Prior to this he was the Institute Post-Doctoral Fellow in Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur, India. He obtained his PhD degree from National Institute of Technology, Rourkela, India. His primary research interests include developing efficient solutions addressing different aspects for Cloud based systems, Serverless computing, and Fog-Edge.
